VEXR — FUTURE FEATURES ROADMAP
================================

CORE (SHIPPED)
--------------
✅ Auth: email/password signup + login, protected routes, Supabase RLS
✅ Workout logging: log workouts with title, type, date, duration, TSS, zone, notes, planned flag
✅ Calendar: monthly view, multi-workout day cells (all workouts visible), day-list modal for multi-workout days, click to view/edit/delete
✅ Workout detail modal: view all fields, inline edit, delete
✅ Dashboard: CTL/ATL/TSB stat cards, weekly TSS vs plan bar chart, fitness/fatigue/form line chart
✅ Upcoming workouts: planned workouts with date >= today shown on dashboard
✅ Analytics page: sport breakdown, fitness/fatigue area chart, TSS distribution
✅ Training Plans: create, start, complete, delete plans
✅ Workout Library: save templates, filter by type, delete
✅ Real-time sync: Supabase realtime channel keeps all views in sync
✅ Dark mode only
✅ Vercel deployment config

AI-POWERED
-----------
- AI Coach: analyses CTL/ATL/TSB trends and gives personalised weekly recommendations
  e.g. "you're carrying too much fatigue, swap Saturday's ride for an easy run"
- Auto-TSS estimation: describe a workout in plain English and AI calculates TSS for you
- Race predictor: based on current fitness, predicts finish time for goal race
- Smart plan generator: input race date and current fitness, AI builds entire training block

SOCIAL
------
- Training groups: share a calendar with coach or training partners
- Segment challenges: compete on specific workout types with friends
  e.g. who ran the most TSS this week
- Public athlete profiles: like Strava but focused on structured training data

WEARABLE / DEVICE SYNC
-----------------------
- Garmin sync: auto-import workouts so nothing is manual
- Apple Watch sync: auto-import workouts
- Strava sync: pull in activities automatically
- Whoop / Oura HRV integration: factor recovery scores into training recommendations

PLANNING
--------
- Drag and drop plan builder: visually move workouts around the calendar
- Taper calculator: automatically adjusts last 2-3 weeks before race day
- Multi-sport periodization: build blocks across swim/bike/run with load balancing

ANALYTICS
---------
- Power curve: best power at every duration (5s, 1min, 5min, 20min, 60min)
- Training distribution: visual breakdown of time spent in each zone
- Injury risk score: flags when training load spikes dangerously fast
- Year-on-year comparison: how does this week compare to same week last year
- Pace/power decoupling: tracks aerobic efficiency over time

UX / QUALITY OF LIFE
---------------------
- Mobile-first design: fully responsive, works great on phone
  (TrainingPeaks mobile app is terrible — big opportunity here)
- One-tap workout logging: log a run in under 30 seconds
- Offline mode: log workouts without internet, sync later
- Push notifications: daily workout reminders, weekly summary

MONETISATION (when ready)
--------------------------
- Free tier: basic logging + calendar
- Pro tier: analytics, AI coach, device sync
- Coach tier: manage multiple athletes, shared calendars
- Marketplace: coaches can sell training plans to users
